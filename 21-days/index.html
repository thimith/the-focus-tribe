The Process

How does it work?

01

Set a Clear Goal

Define the one thing you want to achieve in 21 days and connect it to a strong why. A goal without a reason won't survive the hard days.

02

Commit to Yourself and the Group

State your goal publicly. Name your consequence. When others witness your commitment, backing out stops being an option.

03

Track your Progress

Log your daily wins in 30 seconds using our 21-D Tracker App. Everyone sees. No hiding a missed day. No "I'll catch up tomorrow." Consistency compounds.

04

Share Wins and Struggles

Weekly live calls with the full cohort. Real talk: what's working, what's not. The group pulls you forward when you can't push yourself.

Common Questions

Q&A

A 21-day accountability sprint in a small group of 10 people max. You define up to 3 goals, commit to them publicly with a real consequence, track daily, and join 3 weekly live calls with the full cohort. It's not a course. There are no modules to watch. It's a structure that forces you to show up.

Anything with a daily action. Fitness habits, business goals, creative projects, quitting something, building a routine. Past cohorts have done all of it. The key is that your goal has a clear daily action you can track as done or not done.

You choose it. It has to be something real: money, a task you hate, something that genuinely stings. You state it publicly to the group on day one. If you miss a day three times, you pay it. No renegotiating. This is what makes it work.

30 seconds per day to log your check-in, plus 3 live calls of roughly 60 minutes over the 21 days. The time to actually do your goal is on you. The challenge just makes sure you show up for it.

We want you to succeed, and that starts with setting goals that actually fit your life. Not everything has to be daily. You can commit to working out 4x a week, meditating 5 mornings, or logging meals on weekdays. Set the targets that are honest and ambitious for you, not what sounds impressive.

If you slip on a goal? Redemption 1. Slip again, Redemption 2. Three strikes and you forfeit. But if you set your goals right, you won't need them.

If you complete all 21 days, attend every call, and track every day, and honestly got zero value, you get a full refund. No hassle. That's the deal. If you quit halfway, there's no refund; the structure only works if you're in it.

Depends on which path you choose. The Exclusive Cohort is run entirely by Thierry. He's led multiple cohorts, personally tracks every member, leads all three weekly calls, and has been through the challenge himself. Nothing is delegated or automated.

The Skool Community is run by Thierry and Niko. Niko is a life coach who has facilitated an online men's circle and brings a depth of experience in accountability and personal growth. Together they run the weekly calls and keep the community active.
